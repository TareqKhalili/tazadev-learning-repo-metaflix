Frequently Asked Questions

What courses do you offer?

We offer a variety of programming courses, including web development, data science, and mobile app development. Our courses are designed for beginners and intermediate learners, and cover a wide range of topics and technologies.

Signing up for MetaFlex is completely free. You can create an account and start exploring our courses at no cost. However, some courses may require payment to access certain features or content.

No, you don't need any prior programming experience to use MetaFlex. Our courses are designed for beginners, and we provide step-by-step instructions and guidance to help you learn at your own pace.

Yes, our courses are fully responsive and can be accessed on any device, including phones and tablets. We also have a mobile app that you can download for free from the App Store or Google Play.
